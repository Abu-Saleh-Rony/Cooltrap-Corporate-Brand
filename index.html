COOLTRAP CORPORATE

FLEXFIT FACTORY PROGRAM

With our Flexfit Factory Program there’s no limit to
customization. Pick the material, stitching, color, shape and
design application of your choice and receive a one-of-a-kind
product developed from scratch specifically for you. Minimum
order quantity of 576 pieces per design, per cap. Sampling
time of 20 days and production time of 90 days.

DOMESTIC PROGRAM

Our domestic program targets smaller quantity orders by
utilizing our available stock of Flexfit blank caps and our in-
house design and embroidery experts. Choose from
our available variety of cap styles that come in different colors
and materials to have your logo/text embroidered or printed on.
No minimum order quantity. Production time of 10 - 30 days
depending on order size.

BLANKS PROGRAM

We stock a huge variety of blank Yupoong Flexfit caps that
come in styles with flexfit bands, snapbacks and adjustable
backs as well as in different colors, sizes and materials.
Minimum order quantity of 24 pieces per style per color.
